Mexican Pizza


Home

Description


The best dish at Taco Bell! Learn to make it at home before they take it away again!


Ingredients


  • Mexican cheese
  • Taco sauce
  • Taco meat/beans
  • Diced tomato
  • Hard flat tortilla shell

Steps


  1. Cut tomato
  2. Prepare cheese and sauce
  3. Sandwich the meat/beans between two of the flat hard shells
  4. Layer the sauce cheese and tomato on top
  5. Do a backflip
  6. Put in microwave for 5 minutes
